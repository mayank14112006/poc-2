# Pragati Nagar Nigam - Civic Services Knowledge Base

This document contains official procedures, rules, fees, timelines, and contact details for municipal services under the Pragati Nagar Nigam (PNN) Municipal Corporation.

---

## 1. Property Tax Service
### Service Description
Property tax is an annual tax paid by property owners in Pragati Nagar to the Pragati Nagar Nigam. It is collected to maintain civic infrastructure, roads, parks, and street lighting.

### Check Dues and Online Payment
- Owners can check their outstanding property tax dues by visiting the official PNN portal and entering their 10-digit Property Identification Number (PIN).
- Payments can be made online via net banking, UPI, credit card, or debit card.
- A receipt with a unique transaction reference number is generated instantly upon successful payment.

### How to Apply for Assessment / Re-assessment
1. Visit the PNN Civic Center or access the online PNN portal.
2. Fill out Form A (Self-Assessment Form) with property details including plot size, built-up area, construction type, and usage type (residential/commercial).
3. Submit the form along with required documentation.
4. An inspector from the Revenue Department will conduct a physical verification within 7 business days.
5. The final assessment order and tax demand notice will be issued within 15 days of verification.

### Documents Required
- Copy of Registered Sale Deed or Title Deed
- Occupancy Certificate (for new buildings)
- Copy of previous year's tax receipt (if applicable)
- Valid Government ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport)

### Fee Details
- **Self-Assessment Application Fee**: ₹250 (Residential), ₹500 (Commercial)
- **Late Payment Penalty**: 1.5% simple interest per month on the outstanding amount if paid after the annual deadline of August 31st.

### Processing Time
- Online payment updates: Instant (up to 24 hours in case of bank delays)
- New property assessment: 15 to 21 business days

### Contact and Office Details
- **Office**: Office of the Revenue Collector, Room 102, Pragati Nagar Nigam Headquarters, Sector 5.
- **E-mail**: propertytax@pragati.gov.in
- **Helpline**: 1800-345-6001

## 2. Water Bill Service
### Service Description
Provision of clean drinking water, management of water supply lines, billing, and connection services for residential and commercial establishments.

### Bill Payment and Dispute Resolution
- Water bills are generated bi-monthly.
- Disputes regarding incorrect billing (e.g., faulty meter readings) can be registered at the Water Works Department office.

### How to Apply for a New Connection
1. Obtain Form B (Water Connection Request) from the PNN web portal or the Water Department counter.
2. Hire a PNN-authorized licensed plumber to sketch the internal plumbing pipeline layout.
3. Submit the completed application form, plumber's layout plan, and supporting documents.
4. PNN engineers will conduct a feasibility survey within 5 business days.
5. Pay the connection and road-cutting fee (if road-cutting is required for line tapping).
6. PNN staff will lay the pipeline connection and install the water meter within 10 business days of payment.

### Documents Required
- Proof of ownership (Sale Deed or Tax Receipt)
- Authorized plumber's pipe layout diagram
- No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the landlord (if tenant)
- Aadhaar Card of the applicant

### Fee Details
- **Residential Connection Fee**: ₹1,500
- **Commercial Connection Fee**: ₹5,000
- **Road Cutting Charges (if applicable)**: ₹1,200 per meter
- **Late Bill Payment Surcharge**: ₹100 or 5% of the bill amount (whichever is higher)

### Processing Time
- New water connection installation: 15 business days from fee payment.
- Leakage complaint resolution: 24 to 48 hours from registration.

### Contact and Office Details
- **Office**: Water Works Division, Ground Floor, PNN Building, Sector 5.
- **E-mail**: watersupport@pragati.gov.in
- **Leakage Helpline**: 1800-345-6002

## 3. Sanitation and Garbage Service
### Service Description
Pragati Nagar Nigam manages daily doorstep dry and wet waste collection, street sweeping, sewage maintenance, and garbage dumping complaints.

### Doorstep Collection Schedule
- **Residential areas**: Daily collection between 7:00 AM and 11:00 AM.
- **Commercial areas**: Daily collection between 9:00 PM and 12:00 AM.
- Citizens must segregate waste into Green Bins (Biodegradable/Wet) and Blue Bins (Non-biodegradable/Dry).

### How to Register a Sanitation Complaint
1. Open the Pragati Nagar Nigam Citizen Portal or PNN Mobile App.
2. Select the "Sanitation/Garbage Complaint" category.
3. Input description (e.g., missed garbage pickup, overflow public bin, sewer block).
4. Upload a geotagged photo (optional but recommended).
5. Submit the complaint to receive a unique PNN-complaint tracking number.

### Documents Required
- None (Citizen ID or mobile verification is required for tracking)

### Fee Details
- **Monthly Waste Collection User Fee**: ₹100 (Residential), ₹500 (Commercial)
- **Fines for Littering in Public Spaces**: ₹500 for first offence, ₹2,000 for subsequent offences.

### Processing Time
- Missed doorstep pickup resolution: Within 4 hours of complaint registration.
- Street garbage clearing / public bin overflow: Within 12 hours.
- Sewer overflow or blockages: Within 24 hours.

### Contact and Office Details
- **Office**: Public Health and Sanitation Wing, Room 304, PNN Headquarters.
- **E-mail**: sanitation@pragati.gov.in
- **Complaint Hotline**: 1800-345-6003

## 4. Birth Certificate Service
### Service Description
Registration of births occurring within the municipal limits of Pragati Nagar and issuance of official Birth Certificates.

### How to Apply for a Birth Certificate (Online and Offline)
1. Births occurring in hospitals are auto-registered by the hospital administration within 21 days. Citizens can download it online using the Hospital Reference ID.
2. For home births or delayed registrations (beyond 21 days), download Form C (Birth Registration Form).
3. Fill out details: Child's name, parents' details, date, time, and exact address of birth.
4. Submit Form C along with affidavits and doctor's certificate at the Registrar Office or upload via the PNN citizen login.
5. Collect the physical copy or download the digitally signed PDF from the portal once approved.

### Documents Required
- Hospital discharge summary (for hospital births)
- Joint affidavit from parents (for home births)
- School leaving certificate / Age proof of parents
- Address proof of the birth location

### Fee Details
- **Registration within 21 days of birth**: Free of charge.
- **Registration from 22 days to 30 days**: ₹100 late fee.
- **Registration after 30 days up to 1 year**: ₹250 (requires Sub-Divisional Magistrate approval and affidavit).
- **Registration after 1 year**: ₹500 (requires Executive Magistrate Order).
- **Extra printed copies of certificate**: ₹50 per copy.

### Processing Time
- Hospital births: Available for download within 3 business days of hospital upload.
- Delayed registrations / home births: 10 to 14 business days.

### Contact and Office Details
- **Office**: Office of the Registrar of Births and Deaths, Mezzanine Floor, PNN Building, Sector 5.
- **E-mail**: birthregistrations@pragati.gov.in
- **Helpline**: 1800-345-6004

## 5. Trade Licence Service
### Service Description
Mandatory licence issued by Pragati Nagar Nigam permitting businesses to carry out commercial trades, manufacturing, or service activities within its jurisdiction.

### How to Apply for a New Trade Licence
1. Register on the PNN Business Portal and select "Apply for New Trade Licence".
2. Fill in business details: trade name, owner name, business type (category A/B/C), annual turnover range, and physical shop address.
3. Upload structural plans, fire safety certificates, and other documents.
4. PNN Trade Inspectors will inspect the business premises within 10 business days.
5. Upon positive report, pay the calculated annual trade licence fee online.
6. The digitally signed Trade Licence will be issued online within 5 business days of payment.

### Renewal and Cancellation
- Licences are valid for one financial year (April 1 to March 31) and must be renewed by April 30 of the next year.
- Cancellation requests can be submitted online with proof of business closure.

### Documents Required
- Property Ownership documents or registered Lease/Rent Agreement of the premises.
- Fire No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Fire Department.
- GST Registration Certificate or Business Registration Certificate.
- ID and Address Proof of the primary applicant.

### Fee Details
- **General Trade Licence (Retail/Service)**: ₹2,000 per year
- **Industrial/Manufacturing Licence**: ₹10,000 per year
- **Food & Restaurant Licence (requires FSSAI)**: ₹5,000 per year
- **Late Renewal Penalty (paid after April 30)**: 25% surcharge of the licence fee.

### Processing Time
- New Licence approval and issuance: 15 to 20 business days.
- Renewal processing: 5 business days.

### Contact and Office Details
- **Office**: Trade Licensing Department, Room 205, PNN Headquarters.
- **E-mail**: tradelicense@pragati.gov.in
- **Business Support Desk**: 1800-345-6005
